## How SMART questions are an essential tool for software engineeers

Sometimes, a software developer would find themselves lost in a clueless situation where they don't have any idea what to do next. In this situation, what would be the best option for the person? Stack Overflow? Yeah.... prob 9/10 times, but how do you display your worries in a set of words that form an inquiry that would get you most if not all the answers you had hoped to gain? the answer to this is to ask a SMART question on the forum. With the advent of CHATGPT, the imortance of formatting your wor5ds correctly in a question magnified drastically.
