## Beginning

When learning CSS for the first time, I took interest in many aspects of appearance management. This is a personal project I completed in my self study of HTML and CSS. This project has the webpage divided into 3 sections, the first being the title, where the emperor's title is placed, the second being the image/portrait of the emperor along with its caption, and the final section being the information pertaining to key events during his reign.

## Composition

The title is composed of a header and p element stylized with CSS to matach the color palette of the portrait, imperial yellow and gray. The image is a portrait of the emperor centered and bordered with a solid 10px line.

This is the [Github Repo](https://github.com/RichardBzar/HongzhiWebpage) of the project, containing the html (index.html) and the css (styles.css) files.
